About Me

Having graduated from the DePaul University Kellstadt Graduate School of Business in 2014, Eric Korslin is keenly aware of how businesses today can benefit from the solutions brought forth via automation and web development. An influential professor of his, Dr. Sasha Deklava, worked for 30 plus years as a software consultant for IBM and enlightened Eric to the possibilities innate within development.

In addition to his academic and professional pursuits, Eric enjoys spending time with friends and family, live music, and the Chicago Cubs.

Eric graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Madison School of Psychology in 2007.
